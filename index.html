Details

The team members gathered in the school’s conference room and brainstorming ideas for the project. The project aims to provide a fun and interactive learning experience for students.

The team discussed the project’s objectives and the resources that would be required to achieve them. They also discussed the project’s timeline and budget constraints.



After going through a number of ideas and discarding some of them, the team finally settled on the idea of creating a virtual field trip that would enable students to explore the world of the animal kingdom. They decided to design the project to be interactive and educational, allowing students to experience virtual animal encounters through 3D and AR technology.
